# Rival — The River-Kingdom of Veska (example run)

A DM-run power, controlled by `world-sim`. Lighter than the player sheet; exact numbers only
where Veska touches Haldmark.

## Identity
- **Name:** The River-Kingdom of Veska
- **Government / ethos:** martial monarchy; expansionist, river-proud
- **Leader:** King Roderan IV, "the Tide" (canon in lore.md)
- **Region / borders:** across the Veil river, east of Haldmark; controls the upper crossings

## Posture & Agenda
- **Disposition to player:** hostile
- **Current agenda:** rearm and take Saltgate harbor; expected war-ready ~T6
- **Personality / decision style:** patient aggressor — masses strength, strikes when odds favor

## Strength Bands
- **Economy:** Moderate
- **Military:** High — and growing. Forces near the player (the Veil muster):
  | Force | Type | Qty | Equip | Readiness |
  |---|---|---|---|---|
  | Veil Host (forming) | infantry + levy | ~6,000 | 3 | 50 (rising) |
  | River Lances | heavy cavalry | ~1,200 | 3 | 65 |
- **Tech level:** parity with Haldmark
- **Stability:** Moderate (war footing strains the treasury — an exploitable weakness)

## Levers
- **Relation to player:** opinion −30, status hostile
- **Treaties:** none with Haldmark
- **Strategic assets:** the upper Veil crossings; a larger manpower base
- **Known grudges / hooks:** old claim to Saltgate's customs; resents Haldmark's new Veil works

## Notes
Mobilization confirmed T1 (flag `veska_mobilizing`). Each season of muster raises the Veil Host's
readiness; if Haldmark allies with the Adran League or the Kelsdar, Veska may strike early rather
than let the window close.
