# Lore — Genesis Run

## Canon Facts
- World profile: information-age, Earth-like technological baseline with dense sensor grids, cloud governance, autonomous logistics, and contested data sovereignty.
- Climate profile: coastlines are permanently flooded by recurrent storm surge and sea-rise displacement; interior belts have desertified into heat-risk corridors and dust economies.
- Political profile: surviving population clusters are concentrated in fortified iron dome city-states linked by trade convoys, mesh relays, and fragile treaty frameworks.
- Constraint profile: no verified magic, psionics, FTL travel, supernatural entities, or anomalous physics in public record.
- Strategic horizon: ecosystem collapse is forecast within 12 years absent unprecedented stabilization.

## Institutions and Events
- Integrated Automatics Corporation (IAC): player nation, organized as a Corporate Directorate with explicit AI stewardship doctrine.
- Capital node: Arlin, an IAC-administered dome city centered on robotics foundries, server citadels, and water reclamation exchanges.
- Regional norm: city-states recognize corporate charters, emergency security compacts, and tariff arbitration protocols, but enforcement is uneven.
- Current era condition: information warfare, supply chain sabotage, and infrastructure poaching are common instruments of statecraft.

## Named People and Places
- Arlin: capital of IAC and primary governance seat.
- The Littoral Ring: fragmented flooded coastal arc of former megacities, now a salvage and refugee frontier.
- The Red Interior: desertified inland trade expanse shaped by convoy law and cooling-right disputes.

## Continuity Hooks
- Which neighboring domes accept AI stewardship legitimacy and which reject it as corporate autocracy?
- What legacy obligations bind Arlin to displaced coastal populations now in transit camps?
- Which treaty clauses could fracture first under water scarcity and data embargo pressures?
