# Run Config - Biopunk

## Difficulty
- **Difficulty:** hard

## Simulation Depth
- **Depth:** deep

## Turn Cadence
- **One turn =** a season
- **Auto-advance world:** yes

## Randomness
- **Randomness level:** medium

## Tone
- **Narrative tone:** gritty-political
- **Content boundaries:** no supernatural intervention; keep consequences grounded in logistics, governance, and social pressure.
- **Advisor flavor:** direct, domain-specific, and occasionally adversarial.

## Victory & Failure Conditions
- **Victory:** keep Arlin sovereign and stable through Turn 48 while reducing ecosphere-collapse exposure by securing sustainable food-water-power balance and at least one durable regional compact.
- **Failure:** dome sovereignty lost, or cascading internal collapse (unrest at systemic crisis with no recovery path).
- **Soft fail handling:** play-on-in-decline

## Session Shape
- **Target length:** campaign ~20+ turns
- **Save cadence:** ledger committed every turn; milestone snapshot every 4 turns.
